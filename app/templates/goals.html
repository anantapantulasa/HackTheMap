Our Goals

HackTheMap is a simple and easy tool to help you find the nearest in-person hackathons near you!

We intend to use web scraping to find hackathons taking place and provide the links to those hackathons!.

Our goal is to bring hackers from local communities together through providing a visual map of all in-person hackathons. Save the hassle of manually searching online or through boring filters on websites, HackTheMap is the most intuitive tool out there!
